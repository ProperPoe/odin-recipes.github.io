Beef Tips

Description

Beef Tips are an underrated meal, that consists of mixing diced up steak with rice and gravy. There are many different ways to make this popular dish, the recipes below provide one way to make this yummy food.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Rice
  • Gravy

Steps

  1. Make rice first
  2. Cut steak into small squares
  3. Cook the steak in a seperate skillet from the rice
  4. Pour gravy in the skillet with the steak
  5. Let gravy and steak marinate
  6. Serve with the rice first, and then pour gravy and steak tips over rice
