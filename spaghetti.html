Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is an Italian favorite, that consists of mixing stringy pasta with beef and tomatoes. There are many different ways to make this popular dish, the recipes below provide one way to make this yummy food.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatoe Sauce

Steps

  1. Make pasta first
  2. Cook the ground beef in a seperate skillet from the pasta
  3. Pour tomatoe sauce in the skillet with the steak
  4. Let tomatoe sauce and ground beef marinate
